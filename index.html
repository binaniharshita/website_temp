About Us


ACM-W supports, celebrates, and advocates internationally for the full engagement of women in all aspects of computer science. The Manipal chapter of ACM-W wishes to carry forward the vision of ACM-W in establishing a society that not only motivates young girls to become zealous programmers but also provides them with adequate resources and the right mentor-ship at every step in their path. We are a research-centric group, striving at inclusion and domain-depth.

What We Do


Coding

Coding is an integral element of who we are! We organize workshop and host online contests to further the understanding of Data Structures and Algorithms of the participants.

Research

Our team has a very passionate set of researches. They fulfill their aim to further the research culture on campus by organizing workshops and talks on research subjects.

Design & Development

We have our own group of enthusiastic graphic designers and web developers who make it a point to make the best looking posters and website content for us.

Mentorship

This stands by far the most unique component of us as an organization. We regularly engage in mentoring and teaching each other new developments in our own areas of expertise.
